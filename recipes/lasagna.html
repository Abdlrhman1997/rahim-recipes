lasagna

Description

This cheese lasagna recipe is an easy vegetarian lasagna with ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses.

Ingredients
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups ricotta cheese
  • 4 eggs
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix ricotta, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; mix well.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 45 minutes.
